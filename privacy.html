PRIVACY POLICY

Anti-Ragging College Picker Extension

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

Last Updated: July 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Anti-Ragging College Picker Chrome Extension ("the Extension", "we", "our") handles your information. The Extension is an open-source project distributed under the MIT License. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The Extension:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • Does not track your browsing history or activity.
  • Does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
  • Does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

3. How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • It reads the list of colleges/universities from the dropdown on the currently active web page.
  • It displays a searchable list inside the extension popup.
  • When you select a college, it sets the value of the dropdown on the page.
  • All processing happens locally on your device — no data ever leaves your browser.

4. Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with the currently active tab and read dropdown contents.
  • scripting: To inject the content script that reads and sets dropdown values on the page.

These permissions are used solely for the Extension's core functionality and nothing else.

5. Data Storage

The Extension does not store any data locally or remotely. Search results are generated in real-time from the page content and are discarded when the popup is closed.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
